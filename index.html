About Us

Welcome to Mandi Sebam

Welcome to Mandi Sebam! We are here to offer the best authentic Yemeni dishes that take you on a journey through the rich flavors of Yemen. From delicious Mandi to unique Mandi, we offer you a one-of-a-kind dining experience in the heart of Istanbul. We use the finest fresh ingredients to prepare each dish with great care and ensure every meal is an unforgettable experience..

We are committed to providing high-quality food that reflects the authentic Yemeni traditions. Whether you're enjoying a grilled meat platter or love spicy dishes, every meal we serve is packed with irresistible flavors.
